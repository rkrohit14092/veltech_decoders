How it works

The appication is build using advanced artificial intelligence technology which smartly recognises the type of leaf disease. This is capable of identifying more than 200 types of commonly found diseases. The best part of the application is that it trains itself with every new image, so hence to increase efficiency.

Abstract.

The increasing enhancement in modern technology and development in the field of agriculture, is one of the most important and emerging field in the engineering. We are still living in the country where we are following the traditional agricultural methods to grow crops and harvest. We have a numbers of automatic robots and machines which makes the farming process easy and convenient but the problem lies in the financial conditions of the farmers who are unable to afford expensive machines. The idea of this project is to make an efficient website that could help the farmers detect the type of disease, harming the crop, also suggesting the appropriate pesticide. This website can also help farmers to know the weather forecast, so that they can properly plan about harvest.

Future aspects

More language can be added to make the website accessible to most people.

Software and features to give tips and smart hacks for agriculture can be added in extension to this project.

With help of trained person we can add a helpline number for technical and agricultural helps.
